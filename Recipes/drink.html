☕ Freshly Brewed Coffee

Description

This simple coffee is a clone of that overpriced name-brand bottled coffee drink I somehow became addicted to. It's also a great way to use the leftover coffee that always ends up getting dumped down the drain the next day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup cold brewed coffee
  • 2 (1 gram) packets granular sucralose sweetener (such as Splenda®)

Directions

  1. Mix milk, coffee, and sweetener together in a glass until sweetener is dissolved.
    2. \
  2. Serve hot and enjoy!
